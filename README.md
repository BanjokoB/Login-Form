How Hardware and software are used within the context of the industry 
Hardware and software are driving a digital revolution in farming and food production, known as Agriculture 4.0 or smart farming, to address challenges like shortage of labour, food security, and environmental sustainability. Hardware provides the physical, automated, and sensing capabilities into the field, while software analyses the resulting data to turn it into actionable decisions that optimize yields and minimize waste.
